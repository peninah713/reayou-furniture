About Reayou Furniture LTD

Who We Are

Reayou Furniture LTD is a modern, forward-thinking furniture company based in Athi River, Kenya. We specialize in creating stylish, comfortable, and functional office furniture designed to inspire productivity and elegance in every workspace.

With a deep passion for craftsmanship and innovation, we blend quality materials with modern design to bring you pieces that are not only beautiful but built to last.

Our Mission

To deliver premium office furniture that enhances workspaces through ergonomic design, aesthetic appeal, and unmatched durability — tailored to suit businesses of all sizes.

Our Vision

To be East Africa’s most trusted name in office furniture — setting the benchmark for innovation, customer service, and design excellence.

Why Choose Us?

  • Custom-made furniture solutions
  • Fast, reliable delivery services
  • Professional consultation & after-sales support
  • Competitive prices without compromising quality
